The GameParallel Pets

Adventure Game Jam 2023

Parallel Pets is a 2D adventure game that was created for an Adventure Game Jam. Our team is still working on perfecting the game. Parallel Pets was created using Godot and GDScript.

Parallel Pets is a top-down 2D game filled with scary dungeons. As a pet, you must protect your family's home while they are at work by going through a portal to a parallel world and fighting through dungeons with different weapons and abilities.

I was able to bring my ideas to life through my code. I learned more about the software of Godot while also utilizing my programming and research skills to adapt to a new language and software.
