The AppAdventurous

WSU Crimson Code Hackathon

Adventurous is an app that was created during the WSU Crimson Code Hackathon 2023 Event, which was held at Washington State University. During this hackathon, I and a team of 3 others worked together to create this application, which resulted in a top 3 finish out of 40+ groups and projects.

Adventurous is an app that allowed users to find certain activities to do in Pullman, Washington. This included athletics, food, entertainment, and study spots.

As we only had 24 hours to work on this, the app ended up being local to our university. However, as a team, we would like to see this app in a global direction, where it can be an alternative to other similar apps that offer activities, since our app allows users to include the specifics in their wants and needs.
